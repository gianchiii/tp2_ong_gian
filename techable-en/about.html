About TechAble

We were born to prove that technology can (and must) be a force for inclusion and equity.

Our Mission

Provide digital tools, inclusive education, and real opportunities to people with disabilities in vulnerable contexts, so they can develop their technological talent and access the digital job market.

Our Vision

A future where disability is not a barrier to professional development in technology. Where every person has the same opportunities to learn, create, and innovate regardless of their abilities.

Our Story

TechAble was born in 2022 in Buenos Aires from a group of technology professionals, educators, and disability rights activists. After years of volunteer work in different organizations, we identified a major gap: while the tech sector was growing exponentially, people with disabilities were being systematically excluded from job and training opportunities.

Today, thanks to the support of allies, volunteers, and donors, we have directly impacted more than 850 people through our training programs, mentorships, and job placement initiatives.

What Drives Us

🌍 Real Inclusion

We don't adapt people to technology. We adapt technology and processes so that all people can fully participate.

🤝 Collaboration

We work alongside companies, universities, civil society organizations, and especially with people with disabilities who are the protagonists of their own development.

💡 Innovation with Purpose

We use the best available technological tools, but always in service of people, not the other way around.

📈 Measurable Impact

Every program has clear outcome indicators. We measure employability, job retention, and participant satisfaction.
