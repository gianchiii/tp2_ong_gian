Accessible training

Code Without Barriers

Accessible web development training program • 2025

What is it about?

An intensive 14-week course where people with visual and motor disabilities learn to build fully accessible websites and web apps. We use tools such as NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver, and adapted keyboards.

2025 results

  • 324 people completed the program
  • 78% got their first tech work experience within 6 months
  • 47 accessible web projects were created and presented at demo day

Testimonial

“I never thought I could work as a frontend developer. Thanks to Code Without Barriers, I am now part of a startup team and earning my first salary in dollars.”
— Sofia R., 29, Cordoba
Mobile technology

Mobile Tech Labs

Traveling technology education • 2025

What is it about?

A specially adapted truck visits rural schools, community centers, and organizations across the country, bringing robotics kits, 3D printing, block-based programming, and accessibility tools.

2025 impact

  • 8 provinces visited
  • 2,840 children and young people joined workshops
  • 41% of participants had some type of disability

Testimonial

“My son had never been able to use a computer. At the Mobile Tech Lab, he built his first robot with adapted parts. It was the happiest day of his life.”
— Maria G., Tomas' mother, Salta
Real employability

Inclusive Digital Mentorships

Employability program • 2025

What is it about?

We connect graduates from our programs with senior professionals from companies such as Mercado Libre, Globant, Auth0, and local startups for 3-month mentorships. The program includes LinkedIn preparation, mock interviews, and portfolio reviews.

2025 results

  • 187 active mentorships
  • 87% job placement rate
  • 64 companies hired program participants

Testimonial

“Mentorship gave me confidence and practical tools. My mentor helped me prepare for technical interviews, and today I work as a QA Engineer at a fintech.”
— Diego P., 34, Buenos Aires
