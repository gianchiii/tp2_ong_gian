What We Do

Three programs that transform lives through accessible and inclusive technology.

Our projects combine high-quality training, personalized support, and direct connection with the technological job market.

Accessible training Adapted technology Real employability

Code Without Barriers

Training • 2025 • +320 participants

Intensive web development program for people with visual and motor disabilities. They learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React using assistive technologies.

Mobile Tech Labs

Education • 2025 • 8 provinces reached

We bring robotics, 3D printing, and programming to schools and community centers across the country, adapting workshops for different types of disabilities.

Inclusive Digital Mentorships

Employability • 2025 • 87% placement rate

We connect people with disabilities who completed our training with senior professionals from tech companies for personalized mentorship and job search support.
